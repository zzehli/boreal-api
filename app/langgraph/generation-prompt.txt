Ingredients: 2 1/4 cups flour, 1 tsp baking soda, 1 tsp salt, 1 cup butter, 3/4 cup sugar, 3/4 cup brown sugar, 2 eggs, 2 tsp vanilla, 2 cups Nestlé Toll House chocolate chips.
